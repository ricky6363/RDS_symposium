About The InCharge

A symposium is a platform to express yourself, make your views known, explore your ideas and compete with the sharpest minds in the engineering circuit, lock horns with the best, all while standing your ground. And now, the wait is over! The EEE department of LICET cordially invites you to STROM, a national-level technical symposium, to provide a platform where budding engineers can evince their technical skills and knowledge. It's said that, "It is not the will to win that makes you a winner, but the will to participate to win which does." And so, join us in this symposium to get a sneak peek at licet, take advantage by acquiring new concepts and enjoy the electrosphere. While you're here, register to win great prizes! The countdown begins now.
